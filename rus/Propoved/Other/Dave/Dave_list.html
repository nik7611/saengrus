I was born at Wellesley Hospital in Toronto, on July 14,16? 1966. On Aug 3, 1966 I was admitted to the care of the Children's Aid Society in Scarborough Ontario. My mother was 21 at my birth, she did not tell my father or her family about my birth. Please contact Robert.
The Parable of the Prodigal Son When I was in Bible College in the 70's, I was part of a male quartet that traveled to churches representing the school. One day I was with the dean getting some promotional material together to leave at the church we were going to that weekend and he saw me take a whole pile of pamphlets and said "You can put some back. We don't want to be prodigal with them." Prodigal? The only place I knew that word from was from one of the parables that Jesus told that had come to be known as the Parable of the Prodigal Son. A son took his cut of his father's estate and spent it in wild living and then came crawling back to daddy when he was broke. Did the dean think that I would run off with these pamphlets and wildly litter the streets with them? I asked him what prodigal meant? "Wasteful." Ah, too many pamphlets means many would be wasted. The young man wasted his share of his father's estate. So we could call Jesus' story The Parable of the Wasteful Son.
