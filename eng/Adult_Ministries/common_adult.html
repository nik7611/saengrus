Adult Ministries


Corps Library              Librarian: Nellie Hann
We have a large collection of materials, available and accessible to everyone, and well used by many. The material includes books on Bible study, Christian living, marriage and family issues, church mission, biographies, Salvation Army History and literature, fiction novels, children’s books and videos and much more!

Ladies Night Out               Leader: Lois McCammond
These events are held quarterly as announced. We feature presentations and special guests that will be inspirational and interesting to all ages. We want to encourage the women of our corps to bring friends and have fun with one another during a time of fellowship and food!

Seniors Fellowship               Leader: Major Ellen Boone
We meet bi-monthly on Wednesdays at 12 noon for 2 hours of fun-filled and informative fellowship. We enjoy visits from special guests as well as special outings once in a while. We celebrate together for special calendar highlights and have 3 gala events per year: Thanksgiving soup & pie, traditional Christmas dinner with all the trimmings and a season end BBQ.

Bible Discovery               Leader: Norm Hann
This is an open Bible study that meets on Sunday Mornings at 9:30am. The scriptures unfold before our eyes, equipping us to be His caring, committed followers. People who worship God and want others to be enfolded into the community of faith.

Discipleship              Leader: Elfreda Pitt-Hetherington
This ministry includes caring for the spiritual needs of the corps as well as enrolling new members into The Salvation Army.

In order to become a member of The Salvation Army, one must take an 8 class course that focuses on the history of the Christian church and The Salvation Army, the doctrines of what we believe and the expectations of our membership. At the end of the course, those who participate are invited to become members of The Salvation Army (Senior Soldiers) in which they make a public commitment to God and the church.
