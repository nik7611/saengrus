Ministry In Motion, a blacklight theatre company, is a non-profit volunteer-based performance troupe. The shows feature extravagant, larger-than-life handcrafted props, painted in bright, fluorescent colours, and great music choreographed to portray a Biblical message.

Ministry in Motion is really thrilled to be starting its third season of production. This outstanding group is a perfect example of creativity at its best!

Check out the upcoming shows, and spread the word about us. We'll be posting our 2007-08 calendar of events in the very new future.
