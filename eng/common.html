Welcome!
The Salvation Army North York Temple
is a Christian community church


The Mission of
The Salvation Army North York Temple
is to develop caring, committed followers
of Jesus Christ who worship God and
work together through the power of
the Holy Spirit to win others
to faith in Jesus Christ.

He who began a good
work in you will carry
it on to completion.
Phil 1:6

Based on the great commandment and the great commission, our mission statement motivates us to reach out to people in our community offering love and acceptance, hope and wholeness. It motivates us to minister to the people of our congregation by promoting fellowship, discipleship, worship and ministry.

As you browse through our website, we hope that you'll discover what you are looking for, and, we also hope you'll give us an opportunity to meet you personally some day.

You are more than welcome to give us some feedback on our website and on your visit to our church. We like to know how we're doing.
